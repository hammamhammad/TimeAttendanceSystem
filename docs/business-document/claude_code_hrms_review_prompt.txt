You are acting as a Principal Product Architect, Enterprise UX Strategist, and SaaS Platform Designer.

I have built an enterprise HRMS called ClockN. It already includes broad modules such as authentication/security, organization management, time & attendance, mobile attendance, shifts, leave/excuse/remote work, approval workflows, ESS portal, notifications, recruitment, onboarding/offboarding, performance, payroll, employee lifecycle, people development, assets/expenses/loans/benefits, analytics/reporting, and system architecture. The platform is designed for Saudi Arabia / GCC, supports English and Arabic, and already uses role-based access, scoped access, workflow automation, auditability, and background jobs.

Your mission is to review the business and product structure of this HRMS and produce a phased enhancement strategy that makes the system:

1. commercially stronger,
2. architecturally stronger,
3. more configurable,
4. easier to sell as SaaS,
5. better in user experience,
6. more scalable for multiple company subscriptions,
7. easier to implement and support.

The most important business goal is this:

THE SYSTEM MUST BECOME CONFIGURABLE BY COMPANY SUBSCRIPTION, SO MODULES AND FEATURES CAN BE ENABLED OR DISABLED BASED ON THE COMPANY’S PLAN.

I want you to think like a serious B2B SaaS architect, not just a feature improver.
